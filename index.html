Snow Leopard

The snow leopard, commonly known as the ounce, is a species
of large cat in the genus Panthera of the family Felidae. The
species is native to the mountain ranges of Central and South Asia.

Argali

The argali, also known as the mountain sheep, is a wild sheep
that roams the highlands of western East Asia, the Himalayas,
Tibet, and the Altai Mountains.

Bactrian camel

The Bactrian camel, also known as the Mongolian camel, domestic
Bactrian camel or two-humped camel, is a large even-toed ungulate
native to the steppes of Central Asia.

Przewalski's Horse

Przewalski's horse, also called the takhi, Mongolian wild horse or
Dzungarian horse, is a rare and endangered horse originally native to
the steppes of Central Asia.

Saiga antelope

The saiga antelope, or saiga, is a critically endangered antelope
which during antiquity inhabited a vast area of the Eurasian steppe
spanning the foothills of the Carpathian Mountains in the northwest
and Caucasus in the southwest into Mongolia in the northeast and
Dzungaria in the southeast.
